Dear Mom,

My torch bearer

In world full of selfish, She is the one who is selfless towards her children The below words are not enough to express her

mama,

  • If Someone asks me whom do you love the most??? I will instantly say My Mom.

    If you ask a married guy whom do you love the most? He will say my Wife, who is going to be mom of his children one day.

    The word Mom is such a beautiful and magical and this beautiful word will last eternally. Mom is the most important member of the family 🏚️. She takes cares of needs of family. She is special to her children. A great teacher, a lovely friend, a great supporter and strict parents. Mom sparks the beauty in the family.

    If somebody loves you more than themselves and unconditionally. They are mom. She starts loving us before we arrived in this world without seeing us. Then imagine how much she would love us after we arrived at this beautiful world.

    What is happiness?

    Seeing my Mom after I arrived home from school or outside.

    When I arrived home back from school or outside. The first word I uttered is where is Mom? Though, I don't have any work with my Mom I always want to see her after I reach home and seeing her after I arrived home from school or outside is the most satisfying things for me.

    God can't be everywhere present for everyone so,he created mother for us.

    She is the gift of God. The beauty in her face never faded away. Having suffered a lot in her own life and still behaving like nothing has happened. How can you do that Mom?

    How can you always generate that charming smile Mom? Your beautiful smile is my strength. Hope to see and be the part of that charming face forever.

    Mom, You know I am 16.

    Now, I can cook food for myself, wash my clothes, make my bed. But still you cooks food for me, you still wash my clothes, you always make my bed?

    Why mom?

    At the time I am writing this it's 11:42 Pm it's almost a new day . Today was my First day in high school I just completed my 10th grade so,now I am in 11th class and today was my first day at college.I was quite Nervous this Morning but it ended up being well today.The weather was pretty aggressive today it was raining harsh during the day time and I still remember that when I was returning home from school in bus there was an female standing in the bus stand in that crazy wind and the rainy weather with an umbrella in her hand and that was my mom.She was there for an Boy who has almost turned 16 years old. She got wet on that cold drop of rain but safely ended up bringing me home without an single drop on my body . < 3

    But the thing here i want to ask to her is why mom why you do that ??

    I mean How can you be such selfless ??

    The person who supports me in my difficult situations. You are my first teacher. Whatever I have learned it's from you or because of you. You are my supporter, my inspiration, my strength, my source of happiness, my world, my everything. Nobody can replace you from my Life. Making you happy is the first priority of my Life.🫶🏻

    I love you Mom ...💛
